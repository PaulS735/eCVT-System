CRITICAL SAFETY NOTES

  • Teensy 4.1 GPIO is 3.3V only — NOT 5V tolerant. Never connect 5V signals directly.
  • Actuator WHITE wire is 0-5V. MUST go through voltage divider before Teensy A1.
  • Relay VCC MUST be 5V (from buck), not 3.3V. Coils won't energize reliably at 3.3V.
  • NEVER drive IN1 and IN2 LOW simultaneously — creates 12V dead short through actuator.
  • 75ms relay deadtime enforced in firmware between direction changes.
  • Star ground topology required — all GND wires meet at ONE common point.
  • 500ms hardware watchdog resets MCU if loop stalls — relays default OFF on reset.
  • 20A inline blade fuse on 12V main feed. Do not bypass.
  • Route power wires (12V, GND) separately from signal wires to reduce EMI.
